Setup Instructions:

How to find LeagueID, espn_s2 and SWID:

1. Log in to your ESPN fantasy league 
2. LeagueID is found in the URL
(https://fantasy.espn.com/... leagueId=XXXXXXXXX ... seasonId=2026)
3. For espn_s2 and SWID,
   Press F12 or inspect page, go to Application > Storage > Cookies
   
   then find the rows for espn_s2 and SWID,
   
5. Copy and paste the items into their corresponding boxes in the SETUP page and click Save.
   
   ** Note: SWID includes the {swirly brackets} **
